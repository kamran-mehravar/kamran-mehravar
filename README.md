## 🚀 About Me

I am a Computer Engineering graduate with a strong background in **software engineering**, **applied AI**, and **intelligent system design**.  
My work focuses on building scalable, real-world systems that combine **AI**, **computer vision**, **IoT**, and **embedded technologies**.

Currently, I work on **transformer-based object detection models**, integrating environmental data such as **weather information** into computer vision pipelines to improve robustness under real-world conditions.

In parallel, I am actively involved in **IoT and Smart Agriculture projects**, working with **sensor-based systems** such as temperature, humidity, and pressure sensors, along with embedded platforms like **Arduino**, to design intelligent monitoring solutions for agriculture and food industry applications.

## 🎓 Thesis

### **Multimodal Object Detection on Thermal Images Using Transformer Architecture and Weather Information**

🔗 [Read Thesis](https://etd.adm.unipi.it/t/etd-02062026-161851/)

This work focuses on improving object detection in thermal imagery by integrating environmental data such as **temperature** and **humidity** into transformer-based architectures.  
The goal is to enhance model robustness under **environmental and seasonal domain shifts**.
